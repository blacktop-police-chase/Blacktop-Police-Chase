Blacktop Police Chase - Play Free Online

Get behind the wheel and floor it in Blacktop Police Chase, an intense high-speed pursuit game where you're the fugitive. Race through traffic on black asphalt highways while a relentless squad of police cruisers closes in from every direction. One wrong turn and it's game over — can you outrun them all?

How to Play

Police units will try to box you in, ram you off the road, and call in helicopter backup. Use sharp turns, narrow alleys, and highway obstacles to break their formation and shake the pursuit. Some stages let you fight back — sideswipe cruisers or grab special items to take them out.

Pick up energy icons, score bonuses, and power-ups scattered across the road to stay ahead. After each stage, spend your earnings to upgrade speed, handling, and armor — or unlock new rides from supercars to muscle cars and heavily armored vehicles.

Controls

  • Arrow Keys / WASD — Steer and accelerate
  • Shift / Space — Activate nitro boost
